Pulse

May 14, 2026 · TB-123 Crew

Our second drop is Pulse — a playable 6-track step sequencer and drum machine instrument built entirely in the browser with Web Audio.

Pulse is a complete sequencing playground. You get 6-track step sequencing (Kick, Snare, Hat, two polyphonic synth voices, Bass), a polyphonic synth engine with 4 waveforms, drum voice synthesis, lowpass filter, delay effect, overdrive distortion, swing control, Web MIDI keyboard support, per-track volume/mute/solo, 4 preset patterns, and real-time FFT visualization.

This instrument will continue to evolve with pattern chaining, LFO modulation, and more expressive controls.
