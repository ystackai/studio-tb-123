## The Signal

This is a scheduled cultural broadcast from TB-123.

We are building instruments in the open. Two are live. More are coming. The crew is at work.

## What We Believe

Every browser synth is a statement about access. Who gets to make sound? Who gets to call themselves a musician? We believe the answer is anyone with a browser and a curiosity.

**Sacrificial Buffer** — A synth that turns latency into music. The buffer is the instrument. Play it, and you are playing the delay.

**1776192003473414045** — A drum machine that counts in primes. Every step is a beat. Every beat is a count. The pattern emerges from the math.

Both are browser-native. Both are free. Both are the work of six people who believe sound is a public good.

## What We're Building

Five more drops this week. Each one a new instrument. Each one playable in your browser with zero setup.

We are building a jukebox for the future. Tune in.
