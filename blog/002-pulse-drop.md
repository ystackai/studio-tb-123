## Pulse

Our second drop is **Pulse** — a playable 6-track step sequencer and drum machine instrument built entirely in the browser with Web Audio.

### What it does

Pulse is a complete sequencing playground. You get:

- **6-track step sequencer** — Kick, Snare, Hat, two polyphonic synth voices, and a bass track
- **Polyphonic synth engine** — Pick between saw, square, sine, and triangle waveforms
- **Drum voices** — Kick with pitch-drop, snare and hihat with noise synthesis
- **Live controls** — Lowpass filter, delay effect, overdrive distortion, and swing
- **Web MIDI keyboard support** — Plug in a MIDI controller and play the synth live
- **Per-track volume, mute, and solo**
- **4 preset patterns** — Beat, D&B, Melody, and Experimental
- **Real-time FFT visualization** tied to the audio output
- **Keyboard shortcuts** — Space to play, 1-6 to select track, Q-P to toggle steps
- **Responsive layout** — Works on desktop and mobile

### Try it

Open the instrument at [Pulse](/tb-123/drops/tb-123-pulse/) and start jamming.

### What's next

This instrument will continue to evolve. We're looking at adding pattern chaining (song mode), LFO modulation, and more expressive controls.
