# Preview — Bunny Orbit

**Preview URL:** `games/bunny-orbit/index.html`
**Local test:** `http://localhost:8080/games/bunny-orbit/index.html`

## What You'll See

1. **Title screen** — "Bunny Orbit" with a starfield background, planets visible, "Launch!" button
2. **Active play** — Hold Space/click/tap to thrust toward planets; release to drift
3. **Planet visits** — Landing on each planet gives fuel and bounces you toward the next
4. **Carrot Moon** — The glowing orange carrot-shaped moon at the far end; reach it to win
5. **Debrief** — Score screen with planets visited, fuel remaining, time, and classification

## Controls

- **Desktop:** Hold `Space` or `ArrowUp` to thrust
- **Mobile:** Tap and hold the 🔥 button
- Release to drift; planets' gravity gently captures you

## Creative Intent

This should feel like a gentle space adventure where you guide a cute bunny astronaut through a starlit void, using short thrust burns to hop between colorful planets on the way to a glowing carrot moon — with quiet wonder and playful delight at each hop.
