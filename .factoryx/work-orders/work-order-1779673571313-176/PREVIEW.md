# Preview - Beat District Dance Battle Game

## Preview URL
The game is served at: games/dance-battle/index.html

From the games index page: games/index.html -> click "Beat District" card.

## How to Play

### Controls
- W/A/S/D - Move forward/left/backward/right
- Shift - Sprint (faster movement)
- Mouse - Look around (pointer lock)
- E - Challenge nearby NPC to dance battle
- Arrow Keys - Input dance rhythm during battle
- Escape - Resume after dance battle result

### Dance Battle
1. Walk up to an NPC (look for colored neon circles on the ground)
2. Press E to start a dance battle
3. Colored arrows fall down 4 lanes
4. Press the matching arrow key when arrows reach the target circle
5. Timing matters: Perfect = 100pts, Good = 50pts, OK = 25pts
6. Win with 500+ total points to unlock the next district

### Visual Design
- Neon-noir city aesthetic with glowing streetlights
- Blocky voxel-inspired buildings with emissive neon strips
- Starry skybox with moon
- NPCs are humanoid shapes with colored auras
- HUD with score, district name, wins counter, and minimap

### Districts
1. Central Plaza (starting area)
2. Neon Quarter
3. Bass Boulevard
